Breakfast Recipe of the Week

"Egg a la Hashbrown"

Acquire these:

  • 1 golden potato
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 1/4 of an avacado
  • Pinch of salt
  • Dash of pepper
  • Seasoning (optional)

Some assembly required:

  1. Drizzle a hefty spoonful of olive oil into a small skillet set over medium heat
  2. Slice your potato into a bowl using a cheese grater
  3. Wash the grated potato and squeeze out all remaining water from the peels
  4. Dice your garlic clove and mix in with the potato peels
  5. Throw the potato/garlic mixture into the skillet, spreading to cover the entire surface evenly
  6. Flip your hash brown after about 3-5 minutes
  7. After another 3-5 minutes of sizzlin', crack and egg overtop the hashbrown
  8. Sprinkle salt, pepper and seasoning to taste
  9. Cook the egg over hard and then remove from pan
  10. Mash your avocado and spread over half of the hash-brown
  11. Place your 2 slices of tomato over the avocado and fold the other half over
  12. Take a picture and post that shit on instagram
